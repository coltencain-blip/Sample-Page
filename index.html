Introducing Our AI Virtual Assistant

📉 Problem Statement

Currently, 33% of users who begin their journey in online banking end up calling our support team. This indicates friction in the digital experience and missed opportunities for self-service.

🎯 Goal

Reduce the digital-to-phone rate to under 30% by the end of the year by empowering members to resolve their issues online with the help of a warm, intelligent AI assistant.

🤖 AI Assistant Overview

Our AI virtual assistant is designed to be warm, helpful, and intuitive. It understands natural language, guides users through tasks, and offers empathetic responses that build trust and confidence.

💬 Sample Member Questions & Responses

Q: I forgot my online banking password.

AI: No worries—we’ve all been there! Let’s get you back into your account. Just click “Forgot Password?” on the login page and follow the prompts. I’m here if you need help along the way!

Q: Can I speak to a representative?

AI: I hear you—and I’m here to help however I can! If there’s something specific you’re trying to do, I’d love to help you solve it right here. But if you’d still prefer to speak with someone, you can visit our Contact Us page to reach a representative.

Q: What are your loan rates?

AI: I’d be happy to help! Here’s a quick snapshot of our current rates: Auto Loans from 4.75% APR, Credit Cards from 13.24% APR, and Mortgages at 5.875% APR. Want to calculate your monthly payment?

🌟 Benefits of AI for Member Support

  • 24/7 assistance with instant responses
  • Reduces call volume and operational costs
  • Improves member satisfaction and retention
  • Provides personalized, contextual help
  • Identifies friction points through analytics

🚀 Go-To-Market Strategy & Plan

Phase 1: Discovery

  • Analyze call transcripts and digital session logs
  • Identify top reasons for digital-to-phone transitions
  • Define assistant use cases and success metrics

Phase 2: Prototyping

  • Build assistant flows for top member questions
  • Design warm, empathetic response templates
  • Test internally with staff and select members

Phase 3: Testing

  • Deploy assistant in a limited digital environment
  • Gather feedback and refine responses
  • Measure impact on call deflection and satisfaction

Phase 4: Launch

  • Roll out assistant across online banking and mobile app
  • Train support staff on assistant capabilities
  • Promote assistant through member communications

Phase 5: Measurement & Optimization

  • Track digital-to-phone rate monthly
  • Analyze assistant usage and feedback
  • Continuously improve assistant flows and content
